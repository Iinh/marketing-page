Why Tipsease?

   It is estimated that service workers lose $5.8 billion in tips annually. Research on workers in three large U.S. cities (Chicago, Los Angeles, and New York) finds that 12 percent of tipped workers had tips stolen by their employer or supervisor. Service industry workers, especially those that work in restaurants and bars, are much more likely to suffer minimum wage violations—meaning that they receive less than the applicable minimum wage—than workers in other industries.
   In the United States, the majority of servers rely on tip to pay their bills. It is absurd to think that the hard-earned tip money from customers won't go to the servers. All of this is because they can’t tip their servers directly. We understand the frustration, and we’re here to change that.

How Tipsease Works

   In a nutshell, Tipsease lets everyone tip their servers directly. As a customer, once logged in, you will be able to search and tip servers using their name, the restaurant they work at, etc. Our website is easy to use and navigate to ensure the smoothest experience for our users. And the most important thing: 100% of your tips go towards the server.
   If you're a server, please don't hesitate to create an account! Having a profile on Tipsease is not just about receiving your tips, but also helping your exposure and maintaining a good relationship to the customers that you work with. It's a win-win! Sign up now to become the change to our service industry.
